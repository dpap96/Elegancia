2023

There is a wide range of art styles and movements, and artists are increasingly using new technologies and digital media to create their works. There is also a growing focus on issues of social justice, activism, and environmentalism in contemporary art.

2016

There was a renewed interest in figuration and representational art, which had been somewhat overshadowed by abstraction and conceptualism in previous decades. Many artists began exploring traditional techniques and subject matter, but with a contemporary twist.

2006

There was a growing interest in street art and graffiti, particularly in urban areas. Artists like Banksy gained popularity for their politically charged and often humorous works that were created in public spaces.

1996

The art world was still heavily dominated by postmodernism, a movement that had gained popularity in the 1980s. This style was characterized by an interest in irony, pastiche, and cultural references, and often challenged traditional notions of art and beauty.
